    Skin Resurfacing

    Most of us wish we could slow down the effects aging has on our looks - and maybe even get a few years back in the process. Dr. Sefrioui uses three non-invasive procedures for skin resurfacing: Fractora and Fraxel . The Jplasma procedure is a revolutionary new technique that uses helium to precisely eliminate layer by layer the skin surface with a much lower temperature than other treatments but with longer down-time. Factora delivers targeted bipolar radio-frequency energy to various selectable depths in the skin while Fraxel is a proven laser technology. All three methods help reverse the visible effects of aging, naturally helping you look as young as you feel.

    Skin Tightening

    The recommended skin tightening treatment varies depending on each patient’s needs but Dr. Tawfik Sefrioui uses two non-invasive procedures for skin tightening: Forma and Exilis-Ultra. Forma uses radio-frequency to stimulate the formation of new collagen and improve the skin’s elasticity for long-lasting and remarkable results. Exilis-Ultra uses ultrasound and Radio-frequency technology to gradually strengthen and tone your skin from deep within.

    Injections & Fillers

    Injections such as Botox, and Dysport have become the most popular minimally invasive method to treat fine lines and wrinkles. Dermal fillers such as Sculptra, Juvederm, Voluma, Restylane Lyft, Restylane Silk, and Radiesse are used to add volume to depleted areas of the face, provide a lift and treat wrinkles and folds. Certain fillers also stimulate your own natural collagen to grow.

    SkinPen is a medical-grade, state-of-the-art precision tool available only through skincare professionals. SkinPen works by creating a series of hundreds of microscopic channels into the dermis to stimulate the skin’s natural production of new collagen and elastin, helping skin to look and feel healthier.
